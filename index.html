Let us learn how to make the breakfast of champions.

Requirements

  • The most important requirement for making pancakes is a kitchen
  • Next you can choose to use either a stove or a griddle
  • If you go the stove route you will need a large pan!
  • Next you need a spatula (MAKE SURE IT IS FLEXIBLE) and a 1/4 cup measuring cup
  • You will just need a whole lot of measuring utensils (measuring cups, table spoons, and tea spoons and what not)
  • You'll need some bowls and plates as well for mixing and for serving
  • Don't forget a whisk!


Now let's move on to the recipe I stole from the AllRecipies website.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk,
    egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle,
    using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
